### Privacy Policy for CodeSense AI

**1. Data Collection and Usage**
To provide AI-powered code assistance, CodeSense AI may collect and transmit the following data:

- **Code Snippets:** The code currently visible in your editor or the specific selection you are working on. This is sent to the AI backend solely for the purpose of generating completions or analysis.
- **Anonymous Usage Statistics:** We collect anonymous data regarding plugin usage (e.g., frequency of requests, latency) to improve performance and stability. No personal identity information is tracked.

**2. Data Storage**
We do not permanently store your source code. Code snippets are processed temporarily during the AI generation process and are discarded afterwards. We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

**3. Your Rights**
You can stop data collection at any time by disabling or uninstalling the plugin.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via GitHub Issues.

### 中文隐私政策

**1. 数据收集与使用**
为了提供 AI 辅助编程功能，CodeSense AI 可能会收集并传输以下数据：

- **代码片段：** 您当前在编辑器中可见的代码或您选中的特定代码段。这些数据仅会被发送至 AI 后端用于生成代码补全或分析结果，不会被用于其他目的。

**2. 数据存储**

- 我们不会永久存储您的源代码。代码片段仅在 AI 生成响应的过程中临时处理，处理完成后即丢弃。

**3. 您的权利**
您可以随时通过禁用或卸载插件来停止数据收集。

如果您对本隐私政策有任何疑问，请通过 GitHub Issues 联系我们。
